About Us

Sean and Alison's story begins amidst the bustling corridors of Havasu Regional Medical Center, nestled in the sun-drenched embrace of Lake Havasu City, Arizona. Sean, diligently monitoring patient EKGs as a dedicated monitor technician, found himself intertwined in the rhythm of the hospital's heartbeat. Meanwhile, Alison, a compassionate respiratory therapist, breathed life into the hospital's atmosphere, much like the vital air she tended to.

Their paths converged in the busy halls, where their professional synergy mirrored the symbiotic relationship between the heart and lungs. As colleagues, they shared the challenges and triumphs of their work, forging a bond built on mutual respect and admiration. Beyond the confines of their duties, Sean and Alison discovered shared interests, including their passion for sports, as they took to the field together as teammates for the hospital's softball team.

As fate would have it, Sean's professional journey led him away from Lake Havasu City, guiding him towards the lush landscapes of Oregon. There, he delved deeper into his expertise in cardiac care, specializing in implantable pacemakers and defibrillators, each heartbeat a testament to his dedication. Eventually, life and other opportunities would take him all over the country.

Meanwhile, Alison found solace and purpose in the serene surroundings of Prescott, Arizona, where she nurtured her two remarkable twin children, Miles and Grace. Despite the missed opportunity to name them after beloved Star Wars characters, their bond was as strong as the Force itself.

Over the years, Sean's path crisscrossed with the arid deserts of Arizona, punctuated by memorable moments like witnessing his beloved New England Patriots triumph over the Cardinals in Phoenix. Inspired by nostalgia and a genuine concern for his old friend, Sean reached out to Alison, sparking conversations that ignited a flame of connection between them.

Their bond, like a desert flower blooming in the harshest of landscapes, grew stronger with each exchange. Sean's visit to Alison and her children, along with their lively trio of dogs, became a turning point in their journey. With hearts aligned and futures intertwined, Sean made the bold decision to relocate once more, this time to Arizona, where their love story unfolded amidst the backdrop of endless skies and endless possibilities.

Together, they now eagerly anticipate the adventures that lie ahead, their love a guiding light through every twist and turn, as they embark on a journey filled with laughter, joy, and the promise of a life lived to the fullest.
